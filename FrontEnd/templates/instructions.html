TO PLAY Tic-Tac-Toe:

  1. Select the Tic-Tac-Toe Button on the homescreen
  2. Use the mouse the click the location on the board you wish to place a marker
  3. A loading circle will appear when the bot is thinking, followed by a move by the bot
  4. Try to get three in row
  5. For more information on the rules of the game, visit Tic-Tac-Toe

TO PLAY Connect4:

  1. Select the Connect4 Button on the homescreen
  2. Use the mouse the click the column on the board you wish to place a marker
  3. A loading circle will appear when the bot is thinking, followed by a move by the bot
  4. Try to get four in row
  5. For more information on the rules of the game, visit Connect4
